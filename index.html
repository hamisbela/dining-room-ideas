Best Practices for Dining Room Design

Create the perfect dining room with these expert tips:

  • Consider table shape and size for your space
  • Choose complementary dining chairs
  • Plan proper lighting (chandelier, pendant lights)
  • Select appropriate wall decor and artwork
  • Add a statement rug to define the space
